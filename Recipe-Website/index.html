  • 1 cup milk (I use 2 %)
  • 1.5 cup heavy cream

  • 1.5 teaspoon agar agar powder reduce to 1.25 teaspoon for a softer texture of panna cotta
  • 1 cup (190g) mango puree
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla bean paste
  • Pinch salt

  • 60 g butter I used kerrygold salted butter while testing and the burfi tasted fine(not salty!). If you are worried, use unsalted butter and add a pinch of salt
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 70 g raw pistachios
  • 75 g granulated sugar
  • 190 g milk powder
  • 40 g pistachio butter unsweetened (see note below on how to use pistachio cream instead)
  • 1 tablespoon rose water or 1-2 drops rose essence

Eggless Masala Chai Cake (With Cream Cheese Frosting)

Ingredients

  • 250 ml whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon loose black tea(unflavored) or use 3-4 tea bags
  • 150 g light brown sugar make sure it's not lumpy
  • 230 g all purpose flour sifted
  • 95 ml oil any neutral oil will work
  • 6 oz cream cheese
  • 2-4 tablespoon heavy cream or as needed to adjust the consistency of frosting
